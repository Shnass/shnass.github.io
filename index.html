A TICKET TO THE FESTIVAL YOU CHOOSE INSTEAD OF BRAVE! FACTORY 2022

In August 2022 we were supposed to celebrate 5-year anniversary of Brave! Factory Festival but this will not happen due to the war. Instead, we invented multi-festival Brave Tickets: if you can not go to non-existing Brave! Factory 2022 you luckily can go to another festival existing with Brave Ticket.

This is a unique collaboration with our colleagues and like-minded people from around the world: we asked festivals we personally like if they would like to share their full tickets with us and they ALL said yes. Wow. Thank you.

60% of the money raised from the sales will be transferred to appropriate funds helping children who suffered in this war. 40% of the money will be used for this charity program production and saved for Brave! festival future production.

So. Buying one of Brave Tickets, you celebrate life and anniversary of Brave! Factory, you support Ukraine and our event. Plus you get a nice wooden card, which reminds us all that being alive is the biggest gift one might have.

Choose your festival and celebrate life.

Sincerely,
Brave! Factory & Closer team
